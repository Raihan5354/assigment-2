Who We Are

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum is a charitable organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Slovak language and culture in South Manchester.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Slovak language education and cultural programs that connect families with their Slovak heritage while fostering a vibrant community in South Manchester.

Our Vision

To be the leading center for Slovak education and cultural preservation in the UK, ensuring future generations maintain strong connections to their Slovak roots.
